Who should be vaccinated first?

While vaccine supplies are limited, 
it is recommended that priority be given to 
health workers at high risk of exposure and older people,
including those aged 65 or older.

Who is the vaccine not recommended for?

People with a history of severe allergic reaction
to any component of the vaccine should not take it.

How efficacious is the vaccine?
The AZD1222 vaccine against COVID-19 has 
an efficacy of 63.09% against symptomatic SARS-CoV-2 infection.
